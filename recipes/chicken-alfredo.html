Chicken Alfredo

Description

Alfredo sauce is always a good idea. Just toss the indulgent sauce with pasta (and maybe some chicken or shrimp) to create a crowd-pleasing meal that even picky eaters will enjoy.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Cream Cheese
  • Garlic Powder
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Milk
  • Fettuccine Noodles
  • Pepper
  • Chicken

Steps

  1. Boil fettuccine noodles.
  2. Cook the chicken.
  3. Melt butter and mix in cream cheese, parmesan cheese, milk. Flavor with pepper and garlic powder.
  4. Toss all ingredients together.
