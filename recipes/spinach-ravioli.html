Spinach Ravioli

Description

A delicious combination of chicken and spinach make a wonderful Italian meal. Make sure to have plenty of freshly grated Asiago cheese to top these ravioli.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Salt and pepper
  • Spinach
  • Onion
  • Butter
  • Cheese
  • Garlic powder
  • Marinara sauce

Steps

  1. Mix eggs, water, flour and salt to create dough.
  2. Mix the spinach, butter, onion cheese, salt, pepper and garlic powder
  3. Cut dough into squares. Place mixture inside and seal edges.
  4. Bring pot to boil and cook ravioli.
  5. Toss with marinara sauce./li>
